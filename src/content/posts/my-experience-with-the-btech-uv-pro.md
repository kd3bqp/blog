I've been daily-driving my BTech UV-PRO for a couple of months now, and it's earned its spot clipped to my bag. For the money, there isn't much out there that does this much.

The color touchscreen and app-style interface make it feel more like a tiny radio computer than a traditional HT. Bluetooth and USB-C are small things that turn out to matter every single day — no proprietary cable to lose, no cradle to find.

Battery life under heavy transmit isn't amazing, and the software still gets occasional updates that move things around. But as an everyday VHF/UHF handheld, it's been hard to put down.
